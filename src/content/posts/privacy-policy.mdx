<h2> Privacy Policy</h2>

Last updated: August 6, 2025

Contact: info@webcreator.com.au
<h3> 1. Introduction </h3>

At Hangster Go, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

<h3> 2. Data We Collect </h3>

 ### **Data You Provide:**

   - Name or nickname
   - Profile photo
   - Status
   - Location
   - Email (in case of registration)

### **Data We Collect Automatically:**

   - Approximate location
   - Usage time and frequency
   - Device model, OS
   - Language and timezone

<h3> 3. How We Use Your Data </h3>

### **We use this data to:**

   - Show nearby users on the map
   - Enable messaging
   - Improve and analyze our service
   - Prevent spam or abuse

>> We do not sell your data to third parties.

<h3>  4. Data Sharing </h3>
###
   - Trusted hosting providers
   - Law enforcement (upon official request)
    - External services for media or messaging processing

<h3> 5. Location </h3>

Your location is only used to display you on the map and can be disabled in your device settings.

<h3> 6. Cookies & Analytics </h3>

We do not use third-party trackers. All analytics are anonymous and internal.

<h3> 7. Age Requirement </h3>

Hangster Go is intended for users aged 18 and older. Accounts of underage users will be removed.

<h3> 8. Your Rights </h3>

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Email us at support@hangster.app

<h3> 9. Security </h3>

We use SSL, access control, and backups, but no system is 100% secure.

<h3> 10. Changes to This Policy </h3>

We may update this Policy. Please check this page periodically.
