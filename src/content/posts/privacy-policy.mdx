<h2> Privacy Policy & Terms of Use</h2>

Last updated: August 6, 2025
App: Hangster Go
Contact: info@webcreator.com.au
<h3>1. Introduction</h3>
<p>At Hangster Go, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy and Terms of Use explain what data we collect, how we use it, and the rules for using our app. By using the app, you agree to these terms.</p>

<h3>2. Data We Collect</h3>

<h4>Data You Provide:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Name or nickname</li>
  <li>Profile photo</li>
  <li>Status message</li>
  <li>Email address</li>
</ul>

<h4>Data We Collect Automatically:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Location data (only when you choose to share it)</li>
  <li>Device information (model, OS version)</li>
  <li>App usage data (pages visited, interactions)</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. How We Use Your Data</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Display your profile to other users</li>
  <li>Show your location on the map when you check in</li>
  <li>Enable communication and social features</li>
  <li>Improve app performance and safety</li>
  <li>Respond to user reports and support requests</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. User-Generated Content (UGC) Rules</h3>
<p>We have <strong>zero tolerance</strong> for objectionable content and abusive behavior. Objectionable content includes, but is not limited to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Hate speech, harassment, threats, or bullying</li>
  <li>Sexually explicit or suggestive material</li>
  <li>Violence or promotion of illegal activities</li>
  <li>Spam, scams, or misleading information</li>
</ul>

<h4>Filtering and Moderation</h4>
<ul>
  <li>We use automated filters to detect objectionable content</li>
  <li>All reports are reviewed within 24 hours</li>
  <li>Violating content will be removed and offending accounts banned</li>
</ul>

<h4>Reporting Content</h4>
<p>Users can report objectionable content or behavior via the in-app "Report" feature. All reports are handled within 24 hours.</p>

<h4>Blocking Users</h4>
<p>You can block other users at any time to prevent further contact and visibility. Blocked users are not notified.</p>

<h3>5. Location Sharing</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Location sharing is <strong>optional</strong> and requires your explicit consent each time</li>
  <li>You must manually check in to share your location (no automatic background sharing)</li>
  <li>Declining location access does not prevent you from using other features</li>
  <li>Location data is visible only to other users during your active session</li>
  <li>Location data is deleted or anonymized after it is no longer needed</li>
</ul>

<h3>6. Your Rights</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Edit or delete your profile at any time</li>
  <li>Block or report other users</li>
  <li>Request deletion of your account and data by contacting us</li>
</ul>

<h3>7. Changes to This Policy</h3>
<p>We may update this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use from time to time. If changes are significant, we will notify you in the app or via email.</p>
