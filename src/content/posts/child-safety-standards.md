# Child Safety Standards

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026  
**App:** Hangster Go  
**Contact:** [mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua](mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua)

Hangster Go is committed to protecting children from Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). These standards describe how we address CSAE and comply with child safety laws.

## 1. Child Safety Point of Contact

For any concerns related to child safety, CSAE, or Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), contact our Child Safety team:
[mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua](mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua)
- **Subject:** Child Safety / CSAE Report

We respond to all child safety reports within 24 hours.

## 2. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

Hangster Go complies with applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

- Reporting confirmed CSAM to the [National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline](https://report.cybertip.org/) or relevant regional authorities
- Cooperating with law enforcement when required by law
- Removing and prohibiting content that exploits or endangers minors

## 3. Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

We have zero tolerance for CSAM and CSAE content. Our approach includes:

- **Proactive moderation:** Automated filters and human review to detect and remove CSAM
- **User reporting:** In-app reporting for users to flag harmful content
- **Immediate action:** Content that violates our policies is removed and accounts are banned
- **Reporting to authorities:** Confirmed CSAM is reported to NCMEC or relevant authorities as required by law

## 4. In-App Feedback and Reporting

Users can report objectionable content, abuse, or CSAE-related concerns through:

- **In-app Report feature:** Available when viewing profiles, messages, or content
- **Block and Report:** Users can block other users and submit a report
- **Email:** [mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua](mailto:info@webcreator.com.ua)
for detailed reports

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

## 5. Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Hangster Go prohibits and actively combats:

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Content that sexualizes minors
- Child grooming or solicitation
- Trafficking or exploitation of children
- Sextortion or threats involving minors

We enforce age verification (18+) and remove accounts of underage users. Violations result in permanent bans and reporting to authorities.
